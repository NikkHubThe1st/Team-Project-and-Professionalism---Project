How to Compost

  1. Choose a composting method (backyard, indoor, or municipal).
  2. Add a mix of "greens" (e.g., vegetable scraps, coffee grounds) and "browns" (e.g., leaves, cardboard).
  3. Keep the pile moist but not waterlogged.
  4. Turn the compost regularly to aerate it.
  5. Monitor for proper decomposition, and harvest compost when ready.

Composting reduces waste and enriches soil. Start your composting journey today!
